Traditional Arab Beverages

Refreshing drinks from across the Arab world

Arabic Coffee

Arabic coffee is one of the most famous traditional drinks in the Arab world. It is lightly roasted and flavored with cardamom, often served with dates.

Mint Tea

A refreshing hot tea mixed with fresh mint leaves and sugar. Popular in Morocco and many Middle Eastern countries.

Karkadeh

A sweet hibiscus drink with a deep red color. Served cold in summer and hot in winter.

Tamarind Juice

A delicious sweet and sour drink made from tamarind fruit. Very popular during Ramadan.
