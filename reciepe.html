Chicken shawrma reciepe: I. The Ingredients The Meat: 1 kg Boneless Chicken Thighs: Thighs are essential; breast meat will dry out during the high-heat roasting required for shawarma. The Signature Marinade: Aromatic Base: 5 cloves of garlic (crushed), 1 tbsp fresh ginger (grated). The Tenderizer: ½ cup plain Greek yogurt and juice of 1 large lemon. The Fat: ⅓ cup extra virgin olive oil. The Spice Blend: 2 tsp Cumin 2 tsp Ground Coriander 2 tsp Smoked Paprika (for that "flame-grilled" scent) 1 tsp Turmeric (for the classic golden color) 1 tsp Salt & 1 tsp Black Pepper ½ tsp Cinnamon & ½ tsp Allspice (the "secret" warm notes) ¼ tsp Ground Cloves


the steps: 1. Preparation & Marination Trim excess fat from the chicken but leave some for flavor. Whisk all marinade ingredients in a large glass bowl. Massage the marinade into the chicken. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, though 12–24 hours is ideal for the enzymes in the yogurt and lemon to fully soften the muscle fibers. 2. The "Cheat" Rotisserie Method (Oven) Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F). Line a baking sheet with foil and place a wire rack on top. This allows hot air to circulate under the meat, crisping all sides. Lay the thighs flat. Roast for 20–25 minutes. The Char: Turn on the broiler (top grill) for the last 3–5 minutes until the edges of the chicken turn dark brown and slightly crispy. 3. The Slicing Technique Let the chicken rest for 5–10 minutes to allow the juices to redistribute. Stack 3–4 thighs on top of each other and slice downward into very thin, jagged strips. This creates the mix of juicy interior and crunchy exterior found in professional shops.
Falafel (فلافل) Crispy chickpea balls Ingredients: 2 cups dried chickpeas (soaked overnight) 1 small onion 3 cloves garlic Parsley (a handful) Coriander (optional) 1 tsp cumin 1 tsp salt 1/2 tsp baking soda Oil for frying Steps: Soak chickpeas overnight (don’t cook them). Blend chickpeas, onion, garlic, and herbs until coarse (not smooth). Add spices + baking soda and mix well. Shape into small balls or patties. Fry in hot oil until golden brown. Serve with pita bread and tahini sauce
